Our People

Principal

Mark E. Hammond Sr. is the principal and founder of Hammond & Company of Oklahoma, LLC. Mark’s emphasis is to provide credible sourced information to our clients in the valuation of assets. He assures that appraisal reporting is compliant with current USPAP standards and serves as a project manager in the reporting of large inventories. Mark applies the use of Autodesk technologies in the valuation of assets, cost estimating, and facility processes. He has extensive experience with industrial operations, construction applications, logistical support and the management of large industrial projects.

Mark currently works towards achieving a graduate degree from the University of Oklahoma at the Norman campus and is a graduate of industry training programs in California through Foothill College in Los Altos and San Francisco State University.

Mark maintains a residence in San Francisco and enjoys laboring on his farm in the southeastern area of Oklahoma where he plans to raise Longhorn Cattle and create a legacy for successive generations. Mark is an enthusiast of western history, enjoys being near the Pacific, attending NFL games, and traveling to historical sites.

Director of Operations

Grace Hammond is the Chief Executive Officer and Partner of Hammond & Company of Oklahoma, LLC. Grace oversees all support staff, assures compliance with standards, and provides strategic direction in company operations. Grace is a Senior Associate Director for Operations at the Center for the Economics of Human Development at the University of Chicago. Grace served six years at the Becker Friedman Institute for Research in Economics at the University of Chicago where she was involved in all levels of work required to run a research center.

Grace is a proud alumna of the University of Chicago, where she earned her Bachelor’s degree in Comparative Human Development. Grace loves lakeside living and spends every weekend, no matter the weather, enjoying the sunshine, fresh air and cool waters of Lake Michigan. She enjoys supporting the Blackhawks and attending White Sox games.
